Transportation in Canada

1. Road Transportation

Canada has an extensive road network, and road transportation is a popular and accessible means of getting around. This includes personal vehicles, taxis, rideshare services, and buses.

2. Rail Transportation

Rail transportation plays a crucial role in Canada, connecting cities and regions. Via Rail is the national passenger rail service, offering scenic routes and efficient travel options.

3. Air Transportation

Air travel is essential for long-distance and intercontinental travel. Canada has numerous airports, with major hubs in cities like Toronto, Vancouver, and Montreal, serving both domestic and international flights.

4. Public Transit

Public transit systems operate in many urban areas, providing buses, subways, light rail, and other modes of transportation. These systems contribute to sustainable and efficient city travel.
